# Second Brain Foundation - Vault Template Structure

**Version:** 2.0  
**Type:** Enterprise Multi-Domain Template  
**Purpose:** Production-ready vault structure for organizational knowledge management

---

## Overview

This vault template provides a comprehensive, enterprise-grade structure aligned with the Second Brain Foundation v2.0 architecture. It supports multiple business domains while maintaining semantic interoperability through the unified ontology.

Expanded ontology and Markdown templates aligned to seven focus sectors:
- Manufacturing / Supply Chain
- Healthcare / Veterinary
- IT Services / SaaS / DevOps (ITIL)
- Legal / Risk / Compliance
- Construction / Engineering
- Retail / E-commerce / Advertising
- Agriculture / Food Systems

Use the files in /Templates as starters and file created notes under their corresponding domain folders.

---

## Domain Folder Structure

### 00_Meta - System Configuration
Vault-wide configuration, ontology definitions, and system documentation

### 01_Identity - People & Organizations
Human actors, organizations, teams, and relationship management (CRM)

### 02_Knowledge - Concepts & Research
Theoretical knowledge, research, learning, and conceptual understanding

### 03_Operations - Projects & Execution
Goal-driven work, project management, task execution, and delivery

### 04_Field - Physical Operations
Manufacturing, construction, engineering, and field operations

### 05_Commerce - Business Transactions
Sales, purchasing, financial transactions, and business relationships

### 06_ITIL - IT Service Management
IT operations, service delivery, incident management, and DevOps

### 07_Healthcare - Medical & Veterinary
Healthcare delivery, patient management, and veterinary services

### 08_Legal - Compliance & Risk
Legal matters, regulatory compliance, and risk management

### 09_Agriculture - Food Systems
Agricultural operations, livestock management, and food production

### 10_Retail - Commerce & Advertising
Retail operations, e-commerce, marketing, and customer engagement

### 11_Research - R&D Projects
Research and development, experiments, and innovation tracking

### 12_AI - Artificial Intelligence
AI/ML projects, model development, and automated processing
