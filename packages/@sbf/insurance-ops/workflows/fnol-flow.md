# FNOL to Claim Workflow

## Overview
First Notice of Loss through claim resolution

## Stages
1. **FNOL Intake** - Initial loss report
2. **Claim Creation** - Official claim record
3. **Triage** - Priority and assignment
4. **Investigation** - Inspection and assessment
5. **Estimation** - Damage costing
6. **Approval** - Coverage decision
7. **Repair** - Vendor coordination
8. **Payment** - Settlement
9. **Closure** - Case closed

## Process Flow
1. FNOL submitted by claimant
2. Policy validated
3. Claim record created
4. Adjuster assigned
5. Inspection scheduled
6. Field inspection conducted
7. Damage items cataloged
8. Estimation prepared
9. Repair vendor assigned (if applicable)
10. Payment processed
11. Claim closed

## Automation
- Auto-assign adjuster by workload
- Inspection scheduling assistant
- AI damage categorization
- Cost estimation engine
- Claimant communication templates
- Compliance deadline tracking
