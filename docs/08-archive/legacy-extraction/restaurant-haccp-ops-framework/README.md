# Restaurant & HACCP Operations Framework

**Domain:** Food Service & Safety  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Production Ready

The Restaurant & HACCP Operations Framework ensures food safety compliance, operational consistency, staff accountability, and full traceability from supplier to service under HACCP principles.

## Core Entities (17)

- `food_item` - Food inventory items
- `supplier_profile` - Supplier information
- `delivery_record` - Delivery tracking
- `storage_location` - Storage areas
- `temperature_log` - Temperature monitoring
- `prep_task` - Food preparation tasks
- `recipe_document` - Recipe management
- `equipment_item` - Kitchen equipment
- `cleaning_task` - Sanitation tasks
- `sanitation_schedule` - Cleaning schedules
- `incident_report` - Safety incidents
- `staff_training_record` - Training tracking
- `audit_checklist` - Compliance audits
- `haccp_plan` - HACCP plans
- `critical_control_point` - CCPs
- `corrective_action` - CCP violations
- `waste_log` - Waste tracking

## Key Features

**HACCP Compliance** - Critical control points, monitoring, corrective actions  
**Daily Operations** - Opening/closing checklists, prep lists, service monitoring  
**Temperature Management** - Automated logging, alerts, compliance tracking  
**Cleaning & Sanitation** - Scheduled tasks, compliance verification  
**Supplier Management** - Delivery acceptance, quality scoring, traceability  
**Incident Tracking** - Contamination, illness, equipment failure reporting  
**Audit Readiness** - Automated log compilation, compliance reporting

## Integration Points

- **Health Framework** → Food safety, staff illness tracking
- **Task Framework** → Cleaning tasks, prep assignments
- **Financial Framework** → Supplier invoices, food costs
- **Knowledge Framework** → SOPs, recipes, HACCP docs
- **Relationship Framework** → Suppliers, staff
